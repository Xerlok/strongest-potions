Fortify Magicka

Fortify Magicka increases your total magicka.

Ingredients (need to mix just 2):

  • Ectoplasm
  • Red Mountain Flower
  • Void Salts
  • Briar Heart

Steps:

  1. Add oil to cauldron.
  2. Drop first ingredient in the cauldron and boil for two turns.
  3. Add second ingredient, then boil for one more turn. Let the mixture cool.
  4. When cooled, add the charcoal, but do not boil.
  5. Distill in phial.
