Resist Disease

Resist Disease decreases your chances of contracting a disease.

Ingredients (need to mix just 3):

  • Elves Ear
  • Spider Egg
  • Rotten Flesh
  • Juniper Berries

Steps:

  1. Add wine to cauldron.
  2. Dry and grind the ingredients
  3. Add the ingredients to the cauldron and simmer for two turns.
  4. Add charcoal so that the potion is not poisonous.
  5. Distill in phial.
