Cure Disease

This potion cures all diseases.

Effect will also cure Sanguinare Vampiris, but only within the first three days after infection.

Ingredients (need to mix just 2):

  • Mudcrab Chitin
  • Vampire Dust
  • Hawk Feathers
  • Charred Skeever Hide

Steps:

  1. Grind your ingredients
  2. Add strong alcohol to cauldron and boil
  3. Add ingredients to cauldron
  4. Allow the cauldron to cool.
  5. Distill in phial when finished.
